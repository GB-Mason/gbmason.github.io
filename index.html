My Experience

I’ve worked in product for over 14 years, across fintech, trading platforms, SaaS, and B2B/B2C marketplaces. I’ve held leadership roles including Head of Product and CPO, leading cross-functional teams of 70+ and managing product portfolios across multiple markets.

I’ve worked in companies ranging from early-stage startups to global scale-ups, including IG, Genius Sports, and Oval Money. I’ve built teams from scratch, turned around struggling products, and led platform-wide strategy shifts under pressure.

I'm comfortable switching between high-level strategy and day-to-day delivery. Whether I’m helping a founder shape their roadmap or working with engineers to unblock delivery, my focus is always on practical outcomes.

I now work independently, offering product leadership support to teams who need clarity, momentum, and experience on their side.

Outside Work

When I'm not untangling product challenges, I'm usually found chasing two energetic young kids around, trying to shave seconds off my 5k time (sub-20 min is the goal!), or perfecting my drop shot on the badminton court. My partner and I keep life busy, balanced, and fun.
